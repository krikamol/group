Interested in applying for one of the undergrad/master/PhD internship, PhD, or postdoc positions in our group? 

That is great, but before you submit your application to us, please familiarize yourself with the research conducted in our group, recent publications from our group, venues in which we tend to publish our work, etc. In your application package, we expect to see at least
- CV
- research statement
